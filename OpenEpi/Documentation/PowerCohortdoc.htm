The notations for the formulae are:

Δ = difference of risk of disease between exposed group and non-exposed group;  

κ = ratio of sample size: non-exposed group / exposed group; 

p1= risk of disease among exposed group;

p2= risk of disease among non-exposed group;

p = (p1*n1+p2*n2) / (n1+n2);

q= 1-p;

n1= available sample size among exposed group;
